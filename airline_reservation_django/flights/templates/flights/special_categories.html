Special Categories

Tailored services for passengers with specific needs

Travelling with Children

👶

Infants (Under 2)

Travel on a parent's lap at a reduced fare. One infant per adult. A baby equipment add-on (stroller, car seat) is available for +{{ '10'|currency:selected_currency }}.

🧒

Children (2–11)

Must have their own seat. Children receive the same baggage allowance as adults. We recommend booking seats near family members.

🧑

Unaccompanied Minors (Under 14)

Must be registered with our service at least 48 hours before departure. Our staff will escort the child from check-in to arrival gate.

Passengers with Reduced Mobility

We are committed to making air travel accessible for everyone. Please contact us at least 48 hours before departure to arrange assistance.

♿

Wheelchair Assistance

Available from check-in through boarding. Manual and electric wheelchairs can be transported in the hold free of charge.

🦮

Guide Dogs

Guide and assistance dogs are permitted in the cabin free of charge. Please notify us in advance with certification documentation.

Pregnant Passengers

Week of pregnancyRequirements
Up to 28 weeksNo medical certificate required
29–36 weeksMedical certificate required (issued within 7 days of travel)
37 weeks or moreTravel not permitted
Multiple pregnancyTravel permitted up to 32 weeks with medical certificate
We recommend consulting your doctor before flying during any stage of pregnancy.

Medical Conditions

Passengers with serious medical conditions must carry a doctor's certificate confirming fitness to fly. Oxygen equipment and medical devices must be declared in advance.

Medication in liquid form over 100ml is permitted in the cabin if accompanied by a prescription or medical certificate.
