About Us

Connecting Croatia and Europe since 2010

Who We Are

Airline Reservation is a Croatian regional carrier headquartered in Zagreb. We operate scheduled passenger services connecting Croatia's major cities with key European destinations, with a focus on reliability, comfort and value.

From our main hub at Zagreb Airport, we serve over 30 routes across 15 European countries, carrying more than 2 million passengers annually.

Our Mission

We believe that everyone deserves to fly without compromise. Our mission is to make air travel accessible, comfortable, and straightforward — offering transparent pricing, modern aircraft, and attentive service on every flight.

Sustainability

We are committed to reducing our environmental footprint. Our fleet renewal programme, single-engine taxi procedures and partnership with sustainable aviation fuel suppliers have reduced our per-passenger CO₂ emissions by 18% since 2020.

We aim to reach net-zero carbon operations by 2050 in line with the IATA industry commitment.
