Travel Documents

Everything you need to know before you fly

Required Documents

🛂

Passport

Required for all international flights outside the EU/EEA. Must be valid for at least 6 months beyond your travel date.

🪪

National ID Card

Accepted for travel within the EU and EEA countries. Croatian citizens may use a valid national ID card for EU destinations.

📋

Visa

Depending on your nationality and destination, a visa may be required. Always check entry requirements for your destination country.

✈️

Boarding Pass

Print your boarding pass or have it available on your mobile device. Available after completing online check-in.

Documents for Children

Children travelling with both parents need only their own valid passport or ID card.

If a child is travelling with one parent, a written consent from the other parent is recommended and may be required at border control.

Unaccompanied minors (under 14) must be registered with our service and accompanied by an airline representative throughout the journey.

Children under 2 years (infants) do not require a separate seat but must have a valid travel document.

Important Reminders

Always check the entry requirements of your destination country well in advance of travel. Requirements can change at short notice.

Carry physical copies of your documents as backup. Lost or expired documents may result in denied boarding.

We are not responsible for passengers denied boarding due to missing or invalid travel documents.
