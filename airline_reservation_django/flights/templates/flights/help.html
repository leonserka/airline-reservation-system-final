Help & FAQ

Answers to the most common questions

Booking

How do I book a flight?
Use the search tool on the home page or go to Flights. Select your departure and destination, choose a date, then follow the 5-step booking process: passengers → class → seats → extras → payment.
Can I book a round-trip flight?
Yes. On the home page or flight search, select "Return" and choose both a departure and return date. Both flights will be booked in one session.
Can I book for multiple passengers?
Yes. When searching, select the number of passengers (up to the available seats). Each passenger's details must be entered separately in Step 1.

Check-in

When can I check in online?
Online check-in is available from 24 hours up to 2 hours before departure. You can check in from My Bookings → About Ticket → Check-in.
What do I need to check in?
You need your first name, last name and ID number exactly as entered during booking. These must match your travel document.
How do I get my boarding pass?
After completing online check-in, go to About Ticket and click "Download Boarding Pass" to get a PDF with a QR code.

Payment

What payment methods are accepted?
We accept payment via PayPal. You can use your PayPal balance, or pay with a debit or credit card through PayPal without creating an account.
Will I receive a receipt?
Yes. A booking confirmation email with a PDF receipt is sent to your email address after successful payment. You can also resend it from My Bookings → About Ticket.
In which currencies can I pay?
We support EUR, USD, GBP, CHF, HUF and CZK. Select your preferred currency from the dropdown in the top navigation bar.

Cancellation & Refunds

Can I cancel my ticket?
Ticket cancellation is available exclusively for Plus class tickets. Go to My Bookings → About Ticket → Cancel Ticket.
How long does a refund take?
After cancellation, your payment status changes to Refunded. The actual refund to your account may take 3–5 business days depending on your payment provider.
What if my flight is cancelled by the airline?
If your flight is cancelled by the airline, all booked passengers are automatically refunded and a cancellation email is sent. Refunds are processed within 24 hours.
