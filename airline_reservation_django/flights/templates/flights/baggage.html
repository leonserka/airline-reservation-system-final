Baggage Information

Allowances, dimensions and policies for your luggage

Cabin Baggage (All Classes)

🎒

Hand Luggage — 7 kg

Included with every ticket. Max dimensions: 55 × 40 × 20 cm. Must fit in the overhead bin or under the seat in front of you.

👜

Personal Item

One small personal item (handbag, laptop bag) is also allowed. Max dimensions: 40 × 30 × 15 cm.

Special Equipment

🏄

Sports Equipment — +{{ 40|currency:selected_currency }}

Surfboards, skis, snowboards, bicycles, golf clubs. Must be properly packaged. Max 23 kg.

🎸

Musical Instruments — +{{ 50|currency:selected_currency }}

Guitars, violins, cellos and similar instruments. Must be in a hard case. Max 23 kg.

👶

Baby Equipment — +{{ 10|currency:selected_currency }}

Stroller, car seat or travel cot. One item per infant. Must be checked at the gate.
